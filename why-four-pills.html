WHY 4 PILLS?
Four pills sounds like a lot of pills to take at once. Why not take less?
THE SHORT ANSWER :
1.Less than four pills will not work. You will get a hangover.
2.Four (or more) pills will consistently prevent a hangover.
THE LONG ANSWER :
One of our five major development criteria was to require only one pill.
PREVIVE development criteria:
1.Prevent hangovers
2.Taken only once
3.Conveniently packaged
4.Small enough to swallow
5.One pill

Our initial formula prevented a hangover but it required users to take eight pills to build up enough nutrients to be effective. We knew this was way too many pills to ask people to take. After considerable testing and refinement, we were able to condense our dosage to six pills and eventually to only four.

However, it was not possible to get reduce the number of pills further without severely compromising the effectiveness of the formula.
